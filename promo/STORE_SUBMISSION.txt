# Privacy & Data Security (Chrome Web Store Disclosure)

## Single Purpose: 
TickTab helps you maintain a clean browser by automatically closing tabs that have been inactive for a user-defined period.

## Permissions Disclosure:
- tabs: 
Required to monitor tab activity, check for audio playback, and programmatically close inactive tabs.

- storage: 
Required to save your inactivity timeout settings and tab activity timestamps locally on your machine.

- alarms: 
Required to schedule efficient periodic checks for inactive tabs in the browser background.

Remote Code: 
None. This extension does not use or fetch any remote code.

User Data: 
No personal data, browsing history, or identifiers are collected, stored, or transmitted.

Privacy Policy: 
https://github.com/maiklindner/TickTab/blob/main/PRIVACY.md

## Test Instructions:
1. Open the options page and set a "Custom" timeout to 1 minute.
2. Ensure the Master Toggle is ON and save settings.
3. Open a separate website in a new tab and focus on it.
4. Switch to a different tab or keep the browser in background.
5. After 1 minute of inactivity, the extension will automatically close the background tab to save memory. (Active and pinned tabs are ignored).
