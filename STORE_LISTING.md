## 🇺🇸 English (Default)

**Title:**
ColdTab - Auto Close Inactive Tabs

**Summary (max 132 chars):**
Automatically closes inactive tabs to save memory and keep your browser clean. Smart enough to survive computer restarts!

**Description:**
ColdTab is a smart and privacy-focused extension that automatically closes tabs you haven't looked at in a while. Unlike many other tab managers, ColdTab survives browser and computer restarts! 

Is your browser constantly cluttered with dozens of tabs you promised yourself you'd read later? ColdTab solves this by silently cleaning up forgotten tabs after a customizable amount of time.

**Features:**
* **Customizable Timeouts:** Choose when a tab should expire – anything from 1 day up to a whole year.
* **Restart Resilient:** ColdTab carefully tracks tab activity in the local storage, so its timers survive computer restarts and browser crashes.
* **Smart Exceptions:** It never closes the tab you currently have open, tabs that are pinned, or tabs that are currently playing audio/video (like Spotify or YouTube).
* **Lightweight:** Uses the native Alarms API to check your tabs periodically without draining your battery or hogging your CPU.
* **Privacy First:** ColdTab operates 100% offline. It never sends any of your browsing data, URLs, or activity to any external server. 
* **Dark Mode Support:** The beautiful settings page automatically adapts to your browser's light or dark theme.
