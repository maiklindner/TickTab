TickTab - Auto Close Inactive Tabs

Short Description (max 132 chars):

Is your browser constantly cluttered with dozens of tabs you promised yourself you'd read later? TickTab solves this by silently cleaning up forgotten tabs after a customizable amount of time.

- Sortable Popup: Arrange tabs by age (ascending/descending) to find what you need faster.

- Privacy First: 100% offline. No browsing data or URLs are ever sent to external servers.
